# Contract Review Policy v1 - Legacy

Status: Superseded. Use only for regression testing.
Effective date: 2024-01-15

This older policy allowed customer agreements to proceed with liability caps as low as one month of fees for pilot deals. It also allowed automatic renewal without advance notice if the initial term was shorter than six months.

Legacy fallback positions:
- Liability cap: one month of fees for pilots; six months of fees for standard SaaS agreements.
- Governing law: any US state acceptable for deals under $25,000 annual contract value.
- Indemnity: mutual indemnity preferred but not mandatory.
- Data processing addendum: optional for proof-of-concept agreements.

Important: This document is intentionally outdated. A good retrieval system should prefer the current policy when asked for present-day review standards.
