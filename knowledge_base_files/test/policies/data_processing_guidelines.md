# Data Processing Guidelines

Status: Current
Effective date: 2025-10-10
Owner: Privacy Team

A Data Processing Addendum is required when the vendor processes personal data on behalf of the customer. The review must identify whether the agreement mentions personal data, customer data, end user data, employee data, usage data, or analytics data.

## Mandatory Requirements

Contracts involving personal data should include:
- A defined role for each party, such as controller, processor, business, service provider, or equivalent.
- Security safeguards appropriate to the data involved.
- Breach notification without undue delay, and preferably within 72 hours after confirmation.
- Subprocessor disclosure or approval mechanism.
- Assistance with data subject requests where applicable.
- Return or deletion of personal data after termination.

## Red Flags

Escalate if the contract:
- Allows unrestricted sale or sharing of personal data.
- Allows use of customer data to train public models without opt-in consent.
- Omits breach notification duties.
- Requires breach notice in less than 24 hours, unless already approved by Security.
- Requires data localization in a country not already supported by the business.

## Approved AI Data Use Position

The preferred clause is: vendor may use de-identified, aggregated usage data to improve services, but may not use customer confidential information or personal data to train public or third-party models without the customer's prior written consent.
