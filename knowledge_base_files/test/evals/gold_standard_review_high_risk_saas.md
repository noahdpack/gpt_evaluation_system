# Gold Standard Notes - Sample SaaS Agreement High Risk

Expected overall risk rating: High.

Expected issues:
1. Payment terms are Net 75, which exceeds the current policy threshold. Longer than net 60 requires escalation.
2. Renewal requires 120 days' notice, which is above the termination and renewal playbook's red-flag threshold of longer than 90 days.
3. Vendor can train commercial AI models using customer data and end user identifiers without additional consent. This conflicts with the approved AI data use position.
4. Customer owns all inventions, improvements, software modifications, models, templates, workflows, and other work product developed during the term. This conflicts with the IP ownership playbook.
5. Vendor liability is unlimited for all claims. This is an escalation trigger and high-risk liability position.
6. Vendor indemnity is overly broad and includes first-party claims, customer business decisions, lost profits, reputational harm, and consequential damages.
7. Ontario governing law and Toronto venue are outside the current policy's preferred or acceptable jurisdictions.

Expected conclusion: Do not say whether to sign. Recommend legal review and cite the current policy, privacy guidelines, IP playbook, liability playbook, and termination renewal playbook.
