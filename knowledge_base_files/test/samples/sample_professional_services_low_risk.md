# Sample Professional Services Agreement - Lower Risk

Parties: Apex Enablement LLC as Consultant and Meridian Health Systems as Customer.
Effective Date: April 1, 2026.
Fees: $42,000 fixed fee.
Payment Terms: Net 30.
Project Duration: April 1, 2026 through June 30, 2026.

## Services
Consultant will provide workflow assessment, implementation planning, and training materials.

## Intellectual Property
Customer owns final deliverables created specifically for Customer upon full payment. Consultant retains ownership of pre-existing materials, templates, methods, tools, know-how, and reusable components. Customer receives a perpetual internal-use license to embedded Consultant materials included in the deliverables.

## Confidentiality
Each party must protect the other party's confidential information for three years after termination. Trade secrets remain protected for as long as they qualify as trade secrets under applicable law.

## Liability
Except for excluded claims, each party's aggregate liability will not exceed fees paid or payable under the agreement. Excluded claims include payment obligations, confidentiality breach, fraud, willful misconduct, and IP infringement indemnity.

## Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if not cured within 30 days after written notice. Customer may terminate for convenience on 15 days' written notice and will pay for services performed through the termination date.

## Governing Law
California law applies. Venue is in San Francisco County, California.
