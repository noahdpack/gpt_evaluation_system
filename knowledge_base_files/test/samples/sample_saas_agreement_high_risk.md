# Sample SaaS Agreement - High Risk

Parties: Northstar Analytics Inc. as Vendor and Blue Harbor Retail LLC as Customer.
Effective Date: March 1, 2026.
Initial Term: 12 months.
Fees: $96,000 annually, invoiced upfront.
Payment Terms: Net 75.

## Services
Vendor will provide access to the Northstar analytics platform and related onboarding support.

## Renewal
The agreement automatically renews for successive one-year periods unless Customer provides written notice of non-renewal at least 120 days before the end of the then-current term.

## Data Use
Vendor may use Customer Data, including transaction records and end user identifiers, to improve Vendor services and train Vendor's commercial AI models. Vendor is not required to obtain additional consent for such use.

## Intellectual Property
Customer owns all deliverables, inventions, improvements, discoveries, software modifications, models, templates, workflows, and other work product developed by Vendor during the term.

## Liability
Vendor's liability is unlimited for all claims arising out of or related to this agreement. Customer will not be liable for indirect damages.

## Indemnity
Vendor will defend and indemnify Customer from all third-party and first-party claims arising from the services, Customer's use of the services, Customer's business decisions, lost profits, reputational harm, and consequential damages.

## Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada. Venue is exclusively in Toronto, Ontario.
