# Sample Mutual NDA - Moderate Risk

Parties: Ember Labs LLC and Cardinal Manufacturing Co.
Effective Date: January 15, 2026.
Purpose: Discussion of a possible commercial relationship involving manufacturing process analytics.
Term: Two years.

## Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes business plans, technical information, financial information, product plans, customer lists, and other non-public information disclosed by either party.

## Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is publicly available, already known without restriction, independently developed without use of Confidential Information, or received from a third party without breach of duty.

## Use and Protection
Recipient may use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and must protect it using at least reasonable care.

## Duration
Recipient's confidentiality obligations expire two years after disclosure. The agreement does not separately address trade secrets.

## Return or Destruction
Upon request, Recipient must return or destroy Confidential Information, except one archival copy may be retained for legal compliance.

## Governing Law
New York law applies. Venue is in New York County, New York.
